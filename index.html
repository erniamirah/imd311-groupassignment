What is Organic

DON'T PANIC ITS ORGANIC...

Organic agriculture is a production strategy that aims to provide food that is ecologically, socially, and economically sustainable. Instead, crop rotation, animal and plant manures, some manual weeding, and biological pest control are used in agricultural systems.

Agriculture that is completely organic Organic agriculture refers to a kind of farming that is environmentally friendly by utilizing less chemicals on the land, such as synthetic fertilizers, which can contaminate rivers. It entails increased wildlife and biodiversity, as well as the avoidance of veterinary treatments like antibiotics in cattle production and genetic manipulation. Animal welfare may also benefit from organic farming, as animals must be kept in more natural, free-range settings

Organic farming and food production is a system. Organic farmers strive to provide high-quality food while also benefiting our whole food system, from humans to the environment, plant health to animal welfare.
